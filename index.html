Introduction

Kitzo ($KITZO) is a community-driven meme coin built on the
Solana blockchain, designed to offer fast transactions, low
fees, and real utility. Unlike traditional meme coins, Kitzo
integrates reflections as rewards, making it a dynamic asset
for both casual holders and active investors.

Vision:

1. Provide real utility beyond memes, focusing on reflections.
2. Foster a strong community through engagement, events, and partnerships.
3. Ensure transparency and security for investors through a fair and open ecosystem.

Why solana

Kitzo leverages the Solana blockchain due to its:
- Ultra-fast transactions (65,000+ TPS)
- Near-zero fees compared to Ethereum

- Growing developer & investor community
